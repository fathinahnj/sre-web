Who are we?

Society of Renewable Energy Indonesia Chapter Universitas Hasanuddin (SRE UNHAS) is a student-led organization based in Universitas Hasanuddin which concerns about new and renewable energy and committed to advancing new and renewable energy in Indonesia.

SICLUS 2024 is here!

SICLUS or the Student Innovation Competition in Climate, Energy, and Sustainability is an annual event organized by the Society of Renewable Energy (SRE) at Universitas Hasanuddin aimed at promoting awareness of climate issues and their solutions to the public, particularly high school and university students.
